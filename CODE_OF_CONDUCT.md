# Code of Conduct

Open CAN Analyzer is committed to a welcoming, safe and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient and specific when giving feedback.
- Welcome people with different backgrounds and levels of CAN experience.
- Discuss technical decisions without personal attacks.
- Protect private captures, DBC files, credentials and customer information.
- Put safety first when discussing transmission, replay or vehicle networks.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information and
unsafe instructions presented as harmless are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the contact method
listed in the repository security settings. Maintainers will review reports promptly,
protect confidentiality where possible and may edit, remove or reject contributions or
temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Project maintainers are responsible for applying these expectations fairly in project
spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
